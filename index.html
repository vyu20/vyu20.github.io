Education

My name is Vanessa and I am 21 years old. I am a senior at San Francisco State University studying business with a concentration in information systems. Along with the courses I take at SFSU, I've been taking additional programming courses at CCSF. The courses include Programming Fundamentals of Java, Data Structures and Algorithms: Java, and Intro to SQL Databases. This semester I am taking Intro to HTML and CSS as well as Intermediate HTML and CSS. I am taking additional CS and CNIT courses to broaden my career opportunities.

The path to studying information systems was not a smooth one. I initially studied Kinesiology in hopes of becoming a physical therapist. After realizing I had to attend physical therapy school after receiving a bachelors degree, my focus changed to becoming a nurse. After taking a course in Chemistry and Anatomy, I soon realized that the medical field was not for me. I decided to push myself out of my comfort zone by styudying something I never could have imagined myself studying. That is how I decided to study information systems.
